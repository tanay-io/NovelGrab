### ✅ 4. Unique Design Elements

**Everything is intentionally designed NOT to look AI-generated:**

1. **Warm Color Palette**
   - Terracotta primary (oklch(0.58 0.14 35))
   - Bronze accent (oklch(0.54 0.1 40))
   - Cream background (oklch(0.98 0.02 70))
   - Inspired by premium print culture

2. **Premium Spacing**
   - Generous gaps signal quality
   - Breathing room on every page
   - Not cramped like app stores

3. **Authentic Typography**
   - Single clean font family (Geist)
   - Clear hierarchy (headings vs body)
   - Professional appearance

4. **Micro-interactions**
   - Subtle border shifts on hover
   - Smooth 300ms transitions
   - No flashy animations
   - Polished, not distracting

5. **Light Theme Only**
   - As requested - no dark mode
   - Clean, inviting interface
   - Warm and welcoming

6. **Premium Book Covers**
   - Custom gradient backgrounds
   - 135-degree angle
   - Warm color variations
   - Hand-crafted feeling
